Egg Fried Rice


Egg Fried Rice is a simple chinese dish which is just what the name implies. It involves eggs and fried rice, but the way it's made can make all the difference.

Ingredients

  • White rice, cooked and chilled
  • Eggs, lightly beaten
  • Scallions & garlic, finely chopped
  • Light soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Salt & white pepper
  • Neutral cooking oil (olive oil isnâ€™t suitable)

Steps

  1. Scramble the eggs
  2. Fry the rice
  3. Combine and season
