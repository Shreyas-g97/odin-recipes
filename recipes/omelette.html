Omelette


An omelette is a dish made from beaten eggs, fried with butter or oil in a frying pan.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons grated cheese, any kind
  • 3 to 4 cherry tomatoes, cut in half and sprinkled lightly with salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped basil, parsley, or herb of your choice

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs
  2. Melt the butter
  3. Add the eggs
  4. Fill the omelette
  5. Fold and serve
